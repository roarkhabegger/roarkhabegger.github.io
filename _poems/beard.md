<pre>
Salt & pepper beard brimming
over white trimming of the blue mask
screening the dry mouth's breath -
particulates stopped like metal
through the airport security checkpoint
agents' sleepy gaze at passport
headshot similarities. I'm sure
the story of how your wrinkled white ass
met your wife is fun
at the Thanksgiving table, but this is terminal
2 and boarding starts in 10 minutes.

That's how stories were traded
before 140 character message limits
and pokes from Diane. I was sweating
through your story until pity
puddling in my chest stained
a picture of you crying in a motel bed
with strewn coke cans and mini-bar-whiskeys
spanning the floor. It's early to learn
a new story - a human story -
unfiltered, without sepia covered cleavage.



</pre>
