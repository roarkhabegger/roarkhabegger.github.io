<pre>
Novelty, like loaves of bread
will stale. It won't crystallize
like honey and hatred. 
Will my brain soften like butter
(as suns slide down the sky)
or will it grow and bear fruit?

I'm having a bout of indigestion
in my head. The words go in -
distilled to wrung towels
by gray matter - and leave
residual characters. Twenty-six
digits to choose from. A
is common but X sighs itself
across sentences. The lumbering giant
drags my train of thought
to an equation - a sentence
in another language -
from another time.

</pre>
